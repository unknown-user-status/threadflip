📖 Data Narrative

Malaysia hosts 3.8 million documented migrants

That's 11% of the population — one of the highest proportions in Southeast Asia. When including undocumented migrants, estimates range from 5 to 7 million total non-citizen residents.

0

Refugees in legal limbo

189,000 refugees are registered with UNHCR in Malaysia. Over 84% are from Myanmar, mostly Rohingya. Yet Malaysia has no domestic refugee law and is not a signatory to the 1951 Refugee Convention.

0

$1.6 billion sent home annually

Migrant workers in Malaysia sent USD 1.61 billion back to their home countries in 2024. Indonesia receives the largest share. These remittances are a lifeline for millions of families across Southeast Asia.
